Bali

Kuta Beach.

Kuta Beach is a renowned tourist destination located on the island of Bali in Indonesia. It's one of the most popular beaches on the island and is situated near the capital city of Denpasar. Kuta Beach is famous for its long stretch of golden sand, providing a picturesque setting for beachgoers. Another thing that attract a lot of people is the breathtaking sunsets. Both locals and tourists gather to watch the sun dip below the horizon.
