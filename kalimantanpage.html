Kalimantan

This National Park is one of the best national parks and home to orangutans. They often seem to hang from tree to tree. The area is around 415,040 hectares. You can do various exciting activities here. There is the Sekonyer River which can be accessed by means of transportation such as boats, klotok. During the trip, you can observe orangutans hanging around happily.

Tanjung Puting National park.

Batu Hapu cave.

This tourist attraction in Tapin Regency offers stunning views of natural stalactites and stalagmites. Through the gap in the cave above, direct sunlight enters and displays the beauty of the cave to the maximum. So it would be better if you come here from morning to afternoon. Even though the facilities offered are not very complete, access to Batu Hapu Cave is easy
