Jawa

Borobudur temple.

Borobudur is a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most important and famous Buddhist temples in the world. It is located on the island of Java, Indonesia, near the town of Magelang. Borobudur is a massive temple complex that dates back to the 9th century and is considered a masterpiece of Indonesian architecture and a symbol of Indonesia's cultural and religious heritage.

Madakaripura Waterfall is a notable natural attraction located in East Java, Indonesia. It is often considered the tallest waterfall in Java and is situated within the Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park, not far from Mount Bromo, a famous volcano.The waterfall is surrounded by lush green canyon walls, adding to its scenic beauty. The environment around Madakaripura is often described as serene and tranquil.
